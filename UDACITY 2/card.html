Grizzly bear

Grizzly bears are large animals, 75% of their diet is comprised of berries, leaves and nuts. They also like to eat fish, rodents and moose.

  • Scientific name: Ursus arctos
  • life span: 28 years
  • Average length: 78.7402
  • colors: cinnamon,blond,white,black

Grizzly bears are fairly new to North America. Though the continent has had big bear species in the past, including the giant short-faced bear, grizzlies moved in only about 50,000 to 100,000 years ago, traveling in from Europe and Asia via the Bering Land Bridge.
